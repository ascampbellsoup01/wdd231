Auto Body Shop Information Website

Site Name: Auto Body Info Hub

Reason: This name of the website as a hub for the body shop information.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide information about auto body shop services, customer reviews, and a contact form. It is designed to instill confidence in customers regarding the repair process and to promote business visibility.

Scenarios

Target Audience Questions:

  • What services does the auto body shop provide?
  • How can I contact the auto body shop for an estimate?

Color Schema

Primary Colors Used:

  • #B30900 - Used for headings, navigation bar background, and button.
  • #FFFFFF - Used for page background and text.

Typography

Fonts Used:

  • Arial - Used for the body text and paragraphs.
  • Georgia - Used for headings to make them stand out.
